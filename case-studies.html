Featured Case Study

50-Employee Office Network Infrastructure

Location: Dar es Salaam, Tanzania

Industry: Financial Services

Timeline: 3 weeks from survey to handover

The Challenge

A growing financial services company needed to upgrade their outdated network infrastructure to support 50 employees across two floors. The existing system suffered from frequent downtimes, slow Wi-Fi, and no centralized security.

Our Solution

  • Structured Cat6 cabling for all workstations
  • 12 Wi-Fi 6 access points for seamless coverage
  • 16-channel CCTV system with 12 cameras
  • Access control for 5 entry points
  • Enterprise firewall and network security
